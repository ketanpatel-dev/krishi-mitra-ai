# 🌾 Krishi Mitra AI

## Project Overview

Krishi Mitra AI is an AI-powered multilingual smart farming assistant designed for Indian farmers.

The goal is to help farmers detect crop diseases, get treatment recommendations, access government schemes, understand crop insurance, and receive AI-powered farming guidance in both Hindi and English.

This platform should be simple enough for rural farmers while being technologically advanced enough for hackathon judging.

---

## Main Features

### Disease Detection

- Upload crop image
- Capture image using camera
- AI disease prediction
- Confidence score
- Severity level

### Disease Information

- Disease name
- Symptoms
- Causes
- Prevention
- Treatment

### Treatment Recommendation

- Pesticides
- Organic treatment
- Fertilizer suggestions
- Water management tips

### AI Chatbot

- Agriculture-focused chatbot
- Crop disease guidance
- Government scheme guidance
- Crop insurance guidance
- Disaster compensation guidance
- Fertilizer recommendations

### Multilingual Support

- English
- Hindi

Important:  
When Hindi is selected, everything should appear in Hindi including:

- Crop names
- Disease names
- Treatments
- Chatbot responses
- Government schemes
- Weather descriptions

### Government Schemes

- PM Kisan
- PM Fasal Bima Yojana
- Kisan Credit Card
- Soil Health Card
- eNAM
- PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana

### Crop Insurance

- Insurance information
- Compensation process
- Claim procedure
- Helpline numbers

### Weather Dashboard

- Current weather
- Rain forecast
- Alerts

### Market Price Dashboard

- Mandi rates
- Crop prices

### Farmer Education

- Farming guides
- Best practices
- Awareness content
