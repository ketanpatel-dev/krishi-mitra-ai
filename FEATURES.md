# 🌾 Krishi Mitra AI - Features

## Core Features

### 1. Crop Disease Detection

Upload or capture crop images and detect diseases using AI.

### 2. Disease Analysis

View disease details including:

- Symptoms
- Causes
- Severity
- Spread pattern

### 3. Treatment Recommendation

Get:

- Pesticide suggestions
- Organic remedies
- Prevention tips

### 4. AI Farming Assistant

Ask questions about:

- Diseases
- Fertilizers
- Irrigation
- Crop selection
- Insurance
- Government schemes

### 5. Hindi & English Support

Complete multilingual support.

### 6. Voice Assistant

Speak directly with the platform.

### 7. Government Scheme Portal

Access information about:

- PM Kisan
- PMFBY
- KCC
- Soil Health Card
- eNAM

### 8. Crop Insurance Guidance

Learn:

- Claim process
- Compensation rules
- Required documents

### 9. Disaster Assistance

Support for:

- Flood
- Drought
- Cyclone
- Hailstorm
- Pest attacks

### 10. Weather Dashboard

Weather updates and alerts.

### 11. Market Prices

Latest mandi and crop rates.

### 12. Farmer Education Center

Guides, awareness articles, and farming best practices.
